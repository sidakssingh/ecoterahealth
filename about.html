At Ecotera Health, we are dedicated to combating the growing threat of microplastic pollution by developing innovative solutions at the intersection of environmental and human health.

We are developing a home microplastics detection kit, paired with a mobile app, enabling individuals to track exposure and contribute to community-driven data for public health and environmental action. Beyond this, we are expanding into diagnostics to understand microplastics' health impacts and therapeutics to mitigate these effects, alongside eco-remediation technologies to address pollution at its source.

With a multidisciplinary team of experts in healthcare, engineering, and environmental science, Ecotera Health is pioneering accessible solutions to create healthier ecosystems and communities.
